The presentations were provided on the current and future direction, activities, initiatives and issues of the key stakeholders in the Air Force operational and M&S communities.Comments and Recommendations:The Services forums provide a unique opportunity to exchange ideas and information with industry, academia and other components in the Department of Defense (DoD).  We recommend that future Services forums should be held separately (not during the same night) to facilitate more interaction among the Services representatives.Average attendance (excluding joint sessions)30 to 40Peak attendance (excluding joint sessions)50Peak attendance (including joint sessions)30 to 40Total papers presented0“No-show” presentations0Other presentations and panel discussions5 presentationsRecommended Reading List nomination:                  2006 FALL SIW OUTBRIEF FORM
