Thales is a leading international electronics and systems group, serving defence, aerospace, security and services markets worldwide. The Group employs 61, 500 people throughout the world and generated revenues of 10.3 billion euros in 2004.Thales provides services ranging from consulting to full IT outsourcing solutions to support its customer's change management programmes.Thales provides training services and a broad range of simulation devices, from desktop trainers to full-flight simulators for civil and military aircraft, helicopters, vehicles and land forces defence systems. The group develops Modelling, Simulation and Synthetic Environment used to design complex systems of many types.
